About This Study

We investigated whether Gestalt perceptual principles (symmetry, closure, continuity) influence logical judgment independently of actual argument validity.

This connects research on Bayesian cognition (Tenenbaum), cultural evolution of reasoning (Sobchuk), and experimental philosophy.
